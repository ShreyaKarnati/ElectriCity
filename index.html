Welcome to ElectriCity!

Mission Statement

Build an understanding of energy and climate change in students in order to help inspire a well-informed future generation.


Vision Statement

Create environmental awareness to the next generation in order to ensure a sustainable future.


Our Website

Our website features information about different types of energy, environmental policy, and actions individuals can take in order to contribute to a greener society. Our interactive simulation allows children to experiment with balancing a city budget while ensuring sufficient power and minimizing carbon emissions.
